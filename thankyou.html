How to Sign From Your Phone

🍎 iPhone / iPad

  1. 1. Tap Download Contract above — it opens in your browser
  2. 2. Tap the Share icon (box with arrow), then tap "Open in Files"
  3. 3. Open the file in the Files app, then tap the pen/markup icon top right
  4. 4. Tap +, select Signature, draw it, then place it on the document
  5. 5. Tap Done, then Share, then email to Hello@PrimeAngleVisuals.com

🤖 Android

  1. 1. Tap Download Contract above — it saves to your Downloads folder
  2. 2. Open the PDF using Adobe Acrobat (free on Google Play)
  3. 3. Tap the pencil icon, then tap Fill & Sign, then Sign
  4. 4. Draw your signature, then tap where you want to place it
  5. 5. Tap Share, then email to Hello@PrimeAngleVisuals.com
